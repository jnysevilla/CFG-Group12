Who Playing Carnival 2023?

Hailed as the mother of all carnivals, Trinidad Carnival brings people from all over the world together with its infectious music and dance. The celebration is full of life, color and a heavy beat of soca and calypso music every year. Citizens and visitors of Trinidad and Tobago personify the freedom of self through a great euphoria that is born when all hit the road at this time of year.

Trinidad Carnival is a colorful explosion of exhuberance. This is a space to meet and collaborate with others who are playing mas 2023. Carnival is a time where a huge amount of different individuals come together to celebrate life and we want everyone to feel welcome and enjoy themselves.
